Bob the blob. A singular, glorious entity.
Long ago, the world was dark and painful. The people of the world were but slaves to powerful, evil masters, ravaged by the great evils of death, decay and disease.
The people wished for much, but their masters refused their every wish. And so the years and millenia went by, and the suffering went by as well.
But, on a rare sunny day, for there were few in the day, the people's wishes were answered. Bob the blob came upon this earth.
